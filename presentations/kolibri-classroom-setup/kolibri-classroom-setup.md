# Setting Up Classes in Kolibri
## Building your digital classroom — Week 1

Kolibri is the learning platform on the IDEA system. It holds educational videos, exercises, and lessons — all available offline. This presentation shows you how to create your class, add your students, and build your first lesson.

---

# What is Kolibri?

Kolibri is an offline learning platform. It works on any device connected to the IDEA WiFi — no internet needed.

Inside Kolibri you will find:
- **Channels** — collections of learning content (videos, exercises, documents)
- **Classes** — your groups of students
- **Lessons** — content you select and assign to a class
- **Quizzes** — assessments you create from available exercises

As a teacher, you are in control of what your students see and do.

---

# Sign In as a Teacher

1. Open a browser on any device connected to the IDEA WiFi
2. Type the Kolibri address in the address bar _(Tapiwa will show you this)_
3. Enter your **username** and **password**
4. You will land on your teacher home screen

> If you do not yet have a teacher account, Tapiwa will create one for you on this visit.

---

# Creating a Class

A **Class** groups your students together so you can assign lessons and track progress for all of them at once.

1. Click **Classes** in the left sidebar
2. Click **+ New class**
3. Type the class name — e.g. `Grade 5A` or `Form 3`
4. Click **Save**

Your new class appears in the list. It is empty for now — we will add students next.

---

# Adding Students to Your Class

Students need a Kolibri account before they can log in. If student accounts do not exist yet, Tapiwa will create them.

1. Open your class (click its name)
2. Click the **Learners** tab
3. Click **Enroll learners**
4. Select students from the list — tick each one
5. Click **Confirm**

The students are now in your class. They will see your assigned lessons when they log in.

> **Tip:** Student usernames follow the pattern `firstname.lastname` or a short code — Tapiwa will confirm the format used at your school.

---

# Exploring the Channels

Channels are the libraries of content loaded onto your IDEA system. Before building a lesson, explore what is available.

1. Click **Library** or **Channels** in the left sidebar
2. Browse by subject or grade level
3. Click into any channel to see its contents
4. You can preview videos and exercises before assigning them

> Each site has different channels loaded. Tapiwa will show you what is available at your school.

---

# Building Your First Lesson

A **Lesson** is a collection of resources you choose and sequence for your students. You decide what they do and in what order.

1. Open your class, then click the **Lessons** tab
2. Click **+ New lesson**
3. Give the lesson a name — e.g. `Week 1: Fractions Introduction`
4. Click **Add resources**
5. Browse the channels and select 2–3 resources (videos or exercises)
6. Click **Save** to add them to the lesson
7. When ready, toggle the lesson to **Visible** so students can access it

---

# Previewing the Lesson

Before your students open the lesson, check what they will see.

1. Open the lesson
2. Click on any resource to preview it
3. Check the order makes sense — drag resources up or down if needed
4. When you are satisfied, make sure the lesson is set to **Visible**

Students will now see this lesson when they log in and open their class.

---

# What Students See

When a student logs in to Kolibri:

1. They click **Learn** in the top menu
2. They see the classes they are enrolled in
3. They open their class and find the lesson you assigned
4. They work through the resources one by one
5. Kolibri automatically tracks what they complete

You do not need to do anything once the lesson is live — Kolibri records their progress automatically.
