# Wikipedia Offline — Quick Reference Card
_Keep this card. Use it every week._

---

## Access Wikipedia

1. Open the browser → go to **`[WIKIPEDIA URL]`**
2. No login needed — open to everyone on the network

---

## Search for a Topic

1. Click the **search bar** at the top
2. Type your topic (e.g. `photosynthesis`, `rivers of Africa`)
3. Press **Enter** → click the article you want

> **Tip:** Use simple words. If nothing comes up, try a different word.

---

## Use an Article in Your Lesson

- Read the **opening paragraph** — it gives a plain summary
- Use the **table of contents** to jump to the section you need
- Point to **images and diagrams** to spark discussion
- Do not copy the article word for word — explain it in your own words

---

## Give Students a Research Task

1. Tell students the topic to search
2. Ask them to: find one article → read it → write down three facts
3. Review together as a class

---

## Common Problems

| Problem | What to do |
|---------|-----------|
| Wikipedia does not load | Check Kolibri and Nextcloud — if all three are down, the server is off |
| Article is too difficult | Use the opening paragraph only, or search for a simpler topic |
| Topic not found | Try different words or a broader topic |
