# Kolibri — Quick Reference Card
_Keep this card. Use it every week._

---

## Log In

1. Open the browser → go to **`[KOLIBRI URL]`**
2. Click **Sign in**
3. Enter your **username** and **password**

---

## Find a Lesson

1. Click **Classes** in the left menu
2. Click your class name
3. Click **Lessons** → click the lesson name to open it

---

## Assign a Lesson to Students

1. Open the lesson → check **Recipients** shows your class
2. Toggle the lesson to **Visible** (button turns blue)
3. Students can now see it when they log in

---

## Create a Quiz

1. Click **Classes** → your class → **Quizzes**
2. Click **+ New quiz** → give it a name
3. Click **Add questions** → browse channels → select exercises
4. Set the number of questions → click **Finish**
5. Toggle **Active** when ready for students to take it

---

## Check Student Progress

1. Click **Reports** in the left menu
2. Click **Classes** → your class
3. Click **Lessons** or **Quizzes** → select the item
4. You see one row per student — who has started, completed, or scored

---

## Common Problems

| Problem | What to do |
|---------|-----------|
| Student can't log in | Go to Class → student name → Edit → reset password |
| Lesson not visible to students | Check Recipients and toggle Visible |
| No data in Reports | Students must log in before using — guest mode is not tracked |
