# How to Log In
## Kolibri and Nextcloud

---

## Step 1 — Connect to the WiFi

1. Click the WiFi icon on your device
2. Look for the network: **`appnet`**
3. Click it to connect — no password needed

---

## Step 2 — Open the IDEA App Page

1. Open **Chromium** (the browser on your device)
2. In the address bar at the top, type:

### `engine-1.local`

3. Press **Enter**
4. You will see a list of all available apps and their status

---

## Step 3 — Open Kolibri

1. Find **Kolibri** in the app list
2. Check that it shows **Running**
3. Click the **Kolibri** link to open it
